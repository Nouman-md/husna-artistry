The Artist

About Husna

Hi, I'm Husna and I'm an Arabic calligraphy artist from India, practicing the art since 2020. My journey with calligraphy began as a personal meditation which eventually evolved into a passionate pursuit of blending traditional scripts with modern forms.

My work explores a range of surfaces — from canvas with acrylics, to watercolor sheets with watercolors, and glossy sheets using traditional qalam. I also enjoy creating textured art, adding depth and dimension to my calligraphy pieces. I also offer custom calligraphy pieces tailored to individual preferences, making every artwork truly unique.
